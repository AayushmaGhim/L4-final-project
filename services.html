Our Services

Tourism

We provide you a good look of the Southern Lalitpur, especially Gotikhel, Manikhel and Kaleshwor. These places have many tourist attractions such as: the Kalshwor Mahadev Temple, where if gone hiking an early morning, a mesmerising view of sunrise can be seen. The Simba falls which is a waterfall comprising of 9 waterfalls of same source. And the Batsrni Dham a sacred temple of many hindu gods and goddesses.

Comfort

Our staffs are very warm and welcoming and always on their toes in case you need any help. Our rooms are very clean and well equipped with all the necesary things and also an attached toilet/bathroom. All of this in a very affordable cost of Rs 1100 per person per night including food, parking and guide bonfie and barbecue.
